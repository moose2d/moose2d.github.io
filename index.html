Getting Started

Learn the basics of astrophotography and get started taking beautiful nightscapes with minimal investment

  • Deep Sky Imaging

    Learn the techniques used to image deep sky objects outside of our own solar system. Capture nebulae and galaxies with striking beauty

  • Lunar, Solar, and Planetary Imaging

    Discover our nearby solar system neighbors. Learn the tricks used by the best planetary, lunar, and solar imagers to capture craters, sun flares, planetary rings, and more

  • Widefield Landscape and Milky Way imaging

    Discover what it takes to capture the night sky with a detailed foreground and detailed shots of the Mikly Way core.

    Get your geek on by learning about the science and mathematics behind astrophotography. Light propogation, photographic principles, sensor operation, and more are covered here
