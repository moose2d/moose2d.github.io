Why "Sojourn"?

What does "sojourn" mean? The word sojourn refers to a temporary stay somewhere. In the timeline of the universe, life will only occupy an unbelievably small fraction of it (watch this video by MelodySheep on YouTube if you want your mind blown). The majority of time in the universe will be lifeless. As humans, we are lucky to have a short sojourn in space during which we can marvel at the beauty that surrounds us. Through astrophotography, we can learn about own world, our nearest neighbors in the solar system, and of distant galaxies and nebula. We can study astronomy, learn about mathematics, optics, light propogation, and engineering all at once. We can travel back in time by imaging distance objects millions of lightyears away, which, in turn, can give us clues about the future of the universe, and of our own future here on Earth.

The Mission of Space Sojourn Astro

Space Sojourn Astrophotography is focused on making the hobby of amateur astrophtography accessible to more people, to sharing the beauty of our universe with more, and creating a community to collaborate, share, and learn from one another without introducing uncessary barriers or complexity. The rabbit hole can be as deep or as shallow as you want, but of course we encourage you to bring a shovel.
